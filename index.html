Thanks for visiting my professional site. The job market is competitive. Swing a stick and you’re bound to hit a CPA. So what else do I bring to the table?

I’m an entrepreneur, a philanthropist, a go-getter, a thinker, a believer, an extrovert, a traveller, and most importantly a learner. My business career began at an early age through local family-owned businesses that, due to their small size, gave me the opportunity to tackle new and exciting challenges at every level. Insuring growth and profitability in our businesses meant building long-term, durable relationships with clients and suppliers of all walks of life – something that I’m proud to say has crafted me into the people-person I am today.

After a few years in public accounting as a staff accountant – or as I like to call it, a “Jack-of-All-Trades” – and conquering the CPA exam, I co-founded Azork – a technology start-up specializing in cloud-based software. Like my previous roles in small business and public accounting, Azork gave me the opporunity to lead multiple roles including raising private investment, managing investor relations, establishing multiple equity valuations, reviewing and negotiating contracts, business development, researching, and managing daily operations.

Whether it’s professional or personal, business development or triathlon training, I take pride in all that I do. My broad experience has taught me that true leadership embodies articulate communication, precise organization (pocket protector not included), establishing goals and strategies to achieve those goals, accountability, and integrity. I invite you to see my leadership experience and résumé by clicking here. Emails and phone calls are always appreciated too! I look forward to chatting.

Cheers,
Nayef
